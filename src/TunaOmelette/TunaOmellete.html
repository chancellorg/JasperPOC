Ingredients for Puffy Tuna Omelette

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup(s) milk
  • 1/8 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 1 tablespoon(s) onion, chopped
  • 1 clove(s) garlic, chopped
  • 1/8 teaspoon(s) black pepper
  • 1 can(s) Grace Tuna, drained and flaked
  • 1 tablespoon(s) Grace Margarine
  • 1/4 cup(s) green sweet pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup(s) yellow sweet pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup(s) Tomato, diced
  • 1/4 cup(s) cheese
